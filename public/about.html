The Downtown Tradition

Founded in 1992, Downtown Donuts began with a simple mission: to provide our community with the freshest, most delicious handcrafted treats possible.

Every morning before the sun rises, our bakers are hard at work rolling dough and preparing our signature glazes from scratch. We believe in quality you can taste and service that makes you feel like family.
